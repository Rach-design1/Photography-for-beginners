Welcome to Photography for Beginners


My Vision

My name is Rachel Lawson and I am a Professional Photographer. I absolutely love Photography, especially Fashion, Portraits and Wildlife.
My vision is to make Photography accessible for all levels and budgets. I want you to to enjoy the site, feel you're learning the very basics of Photography to get you started taking your first photographs.
I have my own studio - Lawsons Studios, based in Cannock Chase, where I hold regular group lessons and I can also tailor indivdual or couple lessons too. My Partner is a Wedding Photographer, so I can also organise Wedding Photography lessons with him.
